Travel

One of my many interests is travel. I hope to visit every country in my lifetime and will take every opportunity I have to see the world. My aim for this year is to visit two new countries. This January I had an amazing trip to Amsterdam. It was a great experience to explore the Netherlands with one of my best friends. We visited the Ice Bar, The Heiniken Museum and took a cruise along the Canals. This was amazing because every year Amsterdam host a light festival where different artists from all over the country design lights representing issues such as climate change, the Holocaust and much more. It was a great trip.

My love for travel definitely sprung from my family. When I was younger my dad planned amazing holidays to France or Italy. I loved to explore different cultures and see hiostoric monuments. I remember being amazed by the leaning tower of pisa in Italy and the Arc de Triomph or Eiffle Tower in France. However, my favourite part of each holiday was enjoying the heat by the pool.

I first flew alone in 2018 when I flew to Munich, Germany to visit my friend. She had previously been a foreign exchange student at my school who I became very close with. We visited so many places all over Nuremberg and Munich. Besides doing all of the touristy things I also got to visit her school and meet her friends. This was a great time in my life.

    Sport

    My favourite sport is swimming. I started swimming from a young age and took lessons at my local leisure center;. After graduating through all the levels of the swim school I began to attend an endurance class called "different strokes". Here I built up endurance and worked on my breathing techniques.

    At the age of 14 I did my first sponsored swim. I swam 1 kilometer in aid of a local rehab facility. This was a really rewarding experience. I fundraised for my swim and had to swim 40 lanes of the standard 25 meter pool. After this I decided to compete in a few mini triathalons. These are events where you run, cycle and swim all in a row. I had great fun in the triathalons that I competed in, however I am not a runner which became very obvious.

    In 2015 I took my first Water Safety course. I took this with my sisters and have made friends from it that I still have to this day. In the meantime I have completed Water Safety 1,2,3 and 4, Endurance and Rescue 1,2 and 3. I only have one more cource to complete before I am a certified Life-guard. Through these cources I have learned underwater CPR, how to rescue people from different water accidents and how to act as a lifeguard in a pool. I have also learned how to help people in situations out of the water such as how to call an ambulance and how to perform CPR.
